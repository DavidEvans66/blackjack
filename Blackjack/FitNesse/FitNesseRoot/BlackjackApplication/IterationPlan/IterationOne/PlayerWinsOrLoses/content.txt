The player wins if the value of their card is greater than or equal to the dealers. Otherwise they lose.

Picture cards (J,Q,K) are worth 10. Ace (A) is worth 11

!4 For Example:
| Player Card | Dealer Card | Player Outcome? |
| 7 | 9 | Lose |
| 10 | 10 | Win |
| A | 2 | Win |
| J | A | Lose |
| K | Q | Win |
| 9 | J | Lose |
| 10 | J | Win |
