!2 Card Values
Picture cards (J,Q,K) are worth 10. Ace (A) is worth 11. Other cards are worth their face value.

!4 For Example:
!| Card Values|
|Rank |Value? |
|2    |2      |
|7    |7      |
|10   |10     |
|J    |10     |
|Q    |10     |
|K    |10     |
|A    |11     |

Other values are illegal, and have no value.
!4 For Example:
!| Card Values|
|Rank  |Value?|
|1     |0     |
|Deuce |0     |
|12    |0     |
|13    |0     |
