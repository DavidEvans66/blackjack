The Game outcome is always expressed from the point of view of the player.
The player wins if the value of their hand is greater than or equal to the dealers. Otherwise they lose.

!4 For Example:
!| Player Outcome                         |
|Player Value|Dealer Value|Player Outcome?|
|7           |9           |Lose           |
|10          |10          |Win            |
|11          |2           |Win            |
|10          |11          |Lose           |
|9           |10          |Lose           |
|10          |9           |Win            |
